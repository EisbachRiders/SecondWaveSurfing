**Eisbach Riders is teaming up with arive**

You know this feeling your surf trip starts tomorrow it’s the evening before and you realize that you have broken your fins in the last surfing session and because you have been busy your forgot to replace them. The local surf shop is already closed and anyway you are running out of the time, you need to be at the airport ab 7:00 am. So what to do??? Get arive and get your fins delivered in under 30 minutes.

Or imagine this: It’s Sunday morning the weather is nice and you decide to go the Eisbach surfing. But bad luck in yesterday’s session you broke your fins. All surf shops are closed and even ordering online would not help you. Here is the solution, get the arive app and get your fins in under 30 minutes.

And there are many of those scenarios because this is life this is real.

In order to deliver the best customer experience for surfers in Munich, Eisbach Riders is teaming up with the Start UP arive. This cooperation is a next step helping us for more environmental friendly and sustainable delivery.

From 09.07.2021 you can order selected Eisbach Riders products via the arive App ([Apple App Store](https://apps.apple.com/de/app/arive-30min-delivery/id1567030084?l=en) or [Google Play Store](https://play.google.com/store/apps/details?id=co.shopney.arivejiiqtapp)).

You can find our Eisbach Riders products in the category Fitness or MUC Essentials.

Arive offers our customers a delivery in less than 30 Minutes to your home address or GPS point. The delivery will be sustainable via bike. This way you can surf at the Eisbach river and in case one of your fin breaks you can order and replace them before you are next in the cue.

Currently there are no delivery costs in the future it will be 2 Euro.
